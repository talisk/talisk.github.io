NefEmbeddedPreview

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 25, 2026

No data collection

NefEmbeddedPreview is a local macOS Quick Look utility for RAW photo files. The app and its Quick Look extensions do not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

Local file processing

RAW files are processed locally on the user's Mac. The app reads embedded JPEG preview data from files the user opens or that Finder asks Quick Look to preview.

No tracking or accounts

NefEmbeddedPreview does not include analytics, advertising, tracking, accounts, or cloud sync.
