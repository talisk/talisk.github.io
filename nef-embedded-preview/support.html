Support

For help with NefEmbeddedPreview, contact soontalisk@icloud.com.

What to include

  • Your macOS version.
  • The camera RAW file extension, such as .nef, .arw, .cr3, .rw2, .pef, or .3fr.
  • Whether the problem appears in Finder thumbnails, Quick Look preview, or the app viewer.

How it works

NefEmbeddedPreview previews embedded JPEG data inside RAW files. It does not develop RAW sensor data and it does not modify the original files.

Finder and Quick Look

After installation, macOS discovers the bundled Quick Look extensions and Finder can use them for thumbnails and Quick Look previews. If Finder has cached an older thumbnail, moving the file, relaunching Finder, or waiting for the cache to refresh can help.

This app helps Finder Quick Look. Apple's Preview app may still report that a RAW file type is unsupported.

Privacy

Files are processed locally on your Mac. NefEmbeddedPreview does not upload photos, require an account, or include analytics or tracking.

This public support site is for a proprietary macOS app. It does not publish the app source code.
