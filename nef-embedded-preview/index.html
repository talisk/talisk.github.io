NefEmbeddedPreview

A macOS Quick Look utility that lets Finder show previews and thumbnails for camera RAW files by reading their embedded JPEG preview data locally.

This public site provides support and privacy information for the proprietary macOS app. The app source code is not published here.

Support Privacy Policy

Local previews

RAW files are processed on your Mac. The app does not upload photos.

Finder focused

Designed for Finder thumbnails and Quick Look preview extensions.

RAW formats

Supports NEF and other RAW formats that include embedded JPEG preview data.
